Restore a MyDay backup

This page refills your database from a myday-….json backup file. Use it after a disaster, on a fresh database. If this is a NEW Supabase project, run sql/myday_setup.sql in its SQL Editor first — the shelves must exist before this page can fill them.
Step 1 · Sign in
On a fresh project you won't have an account yet — use “Create account” with your usual email.
Step 2 · Choose your backup file
Tap here and pick your myday-….json file
Step 3 · Review & restore
Restoring adds or updates rows — it never deletes anything already in the database. Rows are matched by their original id, so running this twice is safe (no duplicates). Everything is written to the account you signed in with.
