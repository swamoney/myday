How to use the export

?
  1. Tap Download all entries — saves myday_all.csv. Includes daily Other Total + Other Detail columns (collapsed view, like Recurring).
  2. Tap Other items log — saves myday_all_other_items.csv (one row per item — use this when you want to pivot or filter by item name).
  3. Open My_Day.xlsx in Excel.
  4. Open the CSVs as separate sheets.
  5. Copy rows from myday_all.csv below the header row of My_Day.xlsx — your monthly totals continue to work.
